Privacy Policy

November 2017

Purpose

This is document sets out the privacy policy that applies to all data and information generated by NewVote users. This Privacy Policy is a live document, to be updated in response to evolving circumstances.

Policy

The mission of NewVote is to break down the barriers that have traditionally impede everyday citizens from directly engaging in political discourse, idea formation and decision making by providing a digital platform that invites, encourages and inspires citizens from across the nation to pool their collective knowledge, skills, wisdom and insights to engage, contribute to and shape the future of the world.

The ownership of all data and information collected on NewVote, whether generated explicitly or implicitly, remains with you, the individual and creator.

In exercising these rights, NewVote will never share or disclose data or information that is identifiable at the level of the individual.

NewVote will ensure the very best security measures are in place to keep safe your data, including personal contact details.

NewVote will eventually make it easy for you to delete your account and immediately remove all your data from the NewVote database, or for you at any time to download all your data into an accessible format. In the meantime, either of these options can be enacted by direct email request.

NewVote will notify you of any changes to this policy, and provide an opportunity for comment and feedback on changes before your continued use of the service.

If you have any queries about this privacy policy, or wish to register a complaint about privacy or the use of your data, please feel free to contact NewVote directly.

What is NewVote?

NewVote is a digital democracy app. Our mission is to provide balanced information on current political issues, goals and solutions in Australia.

NewVote is a dedicated platform for you to browse, search, learn, discuss, suggest and vote on issues, goals and solutions.

We are a nonpartisan nonprofit organisation with one purpose - to enhance democracy and bring it to the 21st century.
